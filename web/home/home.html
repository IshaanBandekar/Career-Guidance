Introduction

In recent times career counselling has got the recognition it needed. Career guidance in India had never been much popular amongst students. However, students and their parents are now more concerned about one’s career choices due to unsuccessful jobs and stressful lifestyle. Now, students are more inclined towards making a career in the field they are more passionate about. And the most reliable way to understand your career choices is career counselling. Our project would provide detailed info about every field such as Science, Commerce and Arts right from 11th to Masters and various scholarship which students are not aware of. Recently the Union Cabinet has approved the new National Education Policy (NEP). The App will help the students understand this new system and plan their career with complete information and proper guidance.

Survey on career options

The survey on awareness of career options among Indian students was conducted among 10000 participants. 93% of the Indians are aware of just seven career options even though there are more than 250 career options that can be pursued in India.
